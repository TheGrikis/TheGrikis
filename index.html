About the dish



Prep Time:

20 minutes

Cook time:

2 hours 20 minutes

Yield:

1 large bowl

Nutrition facts:

1240 calories, 12 grams fat

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1200g chocolate
  • 2g baking powder
  • 10 table spoons powdered sugar

How to prepare


Take a fictional wooden hammer and smash the fictional chocolate in many fictional little pieces, then put it on the stove to melt. Meanwhile start preparing your rice and let them cook for 12 minutes. When done pour your and your rice in a fictional bowl and stir vigorously. Let cool for 2 hours.
